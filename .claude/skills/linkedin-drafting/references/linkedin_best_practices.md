# LinkedIn Best Practices Reference

Quick reference for creating engaging LinkedIn content.

## Optimal Posting Times
- Tuesday-Thursday: 9-11 AM
- Avoid: Weekends, late evenings

## Character Limits
- Optimal: 150-200 words
- Maximum before "see more": ~210 characters (3 lines)
- Hook must work in first 3 lines

## Hashtag Strategy
- Total: 3-5 hashtags
- Mix: 2 broad (100k+ followers) + 2-3 niche (10k-50k)
- Placement: End of post

## Engagement Triggers
**Questions**: End with engaging question
**Lists**: Use bullet points (3-5 items)
**Numbers**: Specific metrics attract attention
**Emojis**: 1-2 maximum, strategic placement

## Hook Formulas
- **Achievement**: "Just accomplished [result]"
- **Question**: "Ever wondered why [problem]?"
- **Statistic**: "X% of people don't know [insight]"
- **Story**: "Last week, I [relatable situation]"

## Tone Guidelines
**Professional**: Corporate updates, industry insights
**Conversational**: Personal stories, lessons learned
**Educational**: Tips, tutorials, how-tos

## What Performs Well
✅ Personal stories with lessons
✅ Behind-the-scenes of projects
✅ Industry insights with unique perspective
✅ Practical tips (listicles)
✅ Engaging questions

## What to Avoid
❌ Corporate jargon
❌ Generic motivational quotes
❌ Excessive self-promotion
❌ Controversial politics/religion
❌ Overhyping claims

## CTA Examples
- "What's your experience with [topic]?"
- "Drop a comment if you've faced this"
- "DM me if you want to discuss"
- "Tag someone who needs this"
